About

My Pathway is a personalised provisional mapping tool for learners with additional needs.

At My Pathway we understand that many learners don't make linear progress, and a 'one size fits all' approach to the curriculum simply isn't appropriate for them. Wouldn't it be better if we could design and implement a more personalised curriculum to teach the skills and knowledge our pupils will need most, rather than the stale and generic content of the National Curriculum?

With My Pathway, schools and families can work together to implement their own engaging and personalised curriculum, underpinned by robust systems for tracking and supporting pupil progress.
